Thank you for your interest!

BarcelonaJS is a monthly event organised by the JavaScript community in Barcelona at the Mobile World Center in Barcelona.

We run regular non-profit events and conferences apart from the monthly event, for example MobileJS which had 9 international speakers, 85 participants and was supported by Mozilla, Adobe, Telefonica and other great companies.

About

WeNode is a one day, two track conference organised by the Barcelona JavaScript community for the community. There will be a track for 30 minute talks and a track for one hour workshops. Tracks alternate with beginner/expert levels, to have interesting material for everyone. Expert talks will go along with beginners workshops and the other way around.

WeNode is the event that gets all node.js developers into a room for an entire day. It is an event to finally get to meet in person and is an opportunity for you to reach a strong technical audience as well as a great community of JavaScript enthusiasts.
